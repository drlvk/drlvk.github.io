Last time we found that for every \(x\in Hi\) and any linear subspace \(\Mi\) there is a closest element of \(\Mi\text{,}\) denoted \(P_{\Mi}x\text{,}\) and that \(x-P_{\Mi}x \in \Mi\perp\text{.}\) Another way to describe \(P_{\Mi}x\) is: the unique element \(x'\in M\) such that \(x - x'\in M^\perp\text{.}\)
