Sometimes one can avoid the loss of significance by rearranging the expression algebraically. For example,

\begin{equation*} \frac{\sqrt{x+h} - \sqrt{x}}{h} = \frac{(\sqrt{x+h} - \sqrt{x})(\sqrt{x+h} + \sqrt{x})}{h(\sqrt{x+h} + \sqrt{x})} = \frac{x+h-x}{h(\sqrt{x+h} + \sqrt{x})} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x+h} + \sqrt{x}} \end{equation*}

Algebraically these expressions are identical, but in Matlab, 1/(sqrt(x+h) + sqrt(x)) produces accurate answer 5.0000e-04 for any of the above values of \(h\text{.}\)
