  1. Creates a matrix \(A\) of size \(20\times 20\) according to the formula \(A_{ij} = 1/(i+j-1)\text{.}\) (This will require a double loop)

  3. Computes the solution of \(Ax=b\) and displays the norm of the difference between \(Ax\) and \(b\text{.}\)
