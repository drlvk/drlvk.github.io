There is another logical choice of solution in a problem like this: one could pick the point on the line that is closest to the origin \((0, 0, 0)\text{,}\) that is the solution of smallest norm . This solution can be computed with pinv(A)*b, which results in [-4.5556; 0.1111; 4.7778]. This approach takes longer because it involves computing the pseudoinverse of \(A\) which is a subject of MAT 532.
