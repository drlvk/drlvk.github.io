Use the formula (22.3.3) to interpolate a few functions such as

  • \(f(x) = 1/(x^2+1)\) on \([-5, 5]\)
  • \(f(x) = \tan^{-1} x\) on \([-7, 11]\)
  • \(f(x) = \cos(2x)\) on \([1, 15]\)
  • \(f(x) = \sin(x^2)\) on \([0, 5]\)
  • \(f(x) = \operatorname{sign} x\) on \([-1, 1]\)

Use \(20\) Chebyshev extreme points (or more if you prefer).
